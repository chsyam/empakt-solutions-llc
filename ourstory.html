Our Story

NexoGlobal The Game Changer in the Global IT Staffing

As a professional IT staffing service, NexoGlobal Solutions Inc began the journey in 2017. Our company was founded to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity. We have our business headquarters in Texas (USA) and Hyderabad (India) Texas. NexoGlobal has a rapid expansion plan, and thus we envision further growth of our business in many other places.

A Trustworthy IT Staffing Service

Our IT staffing service assures recruitment service as per the requirements of the organizations. We understand the demands of clients precisely and provide staffing service accordingly. The holistic approach of our IT staffing solution ensures that companies can take their businesses close to their financial goals.

Candidates undergo various screening processes, and thus we recognize the finest personnel suitable for your company. Apart from quality, we also assure quick services to the businesses. In this fast-paced world, we understand the value of time for our clientele. Ensuring that you find fast and effortless IT staffing service at our company.






A Growing Network

Over the years, we have successfully developed a thriving network of competent, skilled, and passionate IT personnel. We stay in touch with both fresher and experienced IT personnel. According to the requirements of the businesses, we provide quick and hassle-free staffing services.

Employers can choose us for a regular and strategic IT staffing service. We provide staffing on both permanent and contractual basis. Nevertheless, our service is compliant with the labour law of the respective country or state. Thus, businesses should not worry about legal hassles in IT staffing.

On the other hand, job seekers should get in touch with us to find the best opportunities as per their capabilities. We identify your talent through our proven and data driven screening process. We recruit the employees while respecting their skills and expected remunerations.
